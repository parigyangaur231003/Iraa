# Iraa - Conversation & Operations Guide

## How Iraa Works

1. **Wake Iraa**: Say "Hello" or "Hey Iraa"
2. **Have a Conversation**: Iraa will listen and respond to your requests
3. **End Conversation**: Say "Goodbye", "Bye", or "Thank you bye" to exit conversation mode

## Available Operations

### Email
- **Command**: "Send an email" or "Email"
- **What Iraa does**:
  - Asks who to send to
  - Asks what it's about
  - Drafts the email
  - Asks for confirmation
  - Sends or saves as draft

### Calendar Events
- **Command**: "Add to calendar", "Create event", "Schedule"
- **What Iraa does**:
  - Asks for event title
  - Asks for start time
  - Asks for end time
  - Creates the event in your Google Calendar

### Google Meet
- **Instant Meeting**: "Start a meet" or "Create instant meeting"
- **Scheduled Meeting**: "Schedule a meeting"
- **What Iraa does**:
  - Creates the meeting
  - Speaks the meeting link

###  Telegram
- **Send Message**: "Send telegram message"
- **Read Messages**: "Read telegram messages"
- **What Iraa does**:
  - Sends/reads messages via Telegram Bot API

###  Time
- **Command**: "What time is it?"
- **What Iraa does**: Tells you the current time

###  Jokes
- **Command**: "Tell me a joke"
- **What Iraa does**: Tells a clean, professional joke

### Spotify Music
- **Commands**: "Play music on Spotify", "Play [song name]", "Play something on Spotify", "Pause the music", "Resume the music", "Change the music"
- **What Iraa does**:
  - Asks which song or artist you want
  - Reads the top Spotify matches
  - Opens the selected track in your Spotify app or browser
  - Controls the Spotify desktop app on macOS to pause, resume, or skip to the next track

### Small Talk
- **Commands**: "Hello", "Hi", "Good morning", "Good evening", "Thank you", "Bye"
- **What Iraa does**: Responds conversationally

## Conversation Flow Example

**User**: "Hello"
**Iraa**: "I am here. What can I do for you?"

**User**: "What time is it?"
**Iraa**: "The time is 05:06 AM. What else can I help you with?"

**User**: "Send an email"
**Iraa**: "Who should I send the email to?"
**User**: "john@example.com"
**Iraa**: "What should the email be about?"
**User**: "Project update"
**Iraa**: [Drafts email and asks for confirmation]
**User**: "Yes"
**Iraa**: "Email sent successfully! Is there anything else I can help you with?"

**User**: "Goodbye"
**Iraa**: "Goodbye! Feel free to say hello whenever you need me."

## Tips

1. **Speak clearly** - Iraa uses speech recognition
2. **Wait for Iraa** - Let Iraa finish speaking before you respond
3. **Be specific** - Clear commands work better
4. **Natural conversation** - You can have a back-and-forth conversation
5. **Exit anytime** - Say "Goodbye" to end the conversation
6. **Sleep mode** - Say "Sleep mode", "Thank you", or "Stop" to put Iraa to rest
